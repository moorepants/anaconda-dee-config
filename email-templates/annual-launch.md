Dear all,

We are preparing for the 20XX-20XX installation of the [Conda Digital Exam
Environment](https://gitlab.ewi.tudelft.nl/bhmgerritsen/anaconda-dee-config)
for the TU Delft computer labs for general use and for exams.

You have from now until June 30 to report issues from last year and make new
requests for the coming year. **We will freeze the set of installed packages on
July 1, 20XX.**

What you should do:

- Review the
  [README](https://gitlab.ewi.tudelft.nl/bhmgerritsen/anaconda-dee-config/-/blob/master/README.md)
  to remind yourself about the annual process and how to contribute
- Review the [current list of
  packages](https://gitlab.ewi.tudelft.nl/bhmgerritsen/anaconda-dee-config/-/blob/master/construct.yaml)
  and [make requests on
  Gitlab](https://gitlab.ewi.tudelft.nl/bhmgerritsen/anaconda-dee-config/-/issues)
  for anything missing or for version upgrades (**Important: do not assume all
  packages from prior years are present and check for packages not explicitly
  in the construct.yaml you may rely on!**)
- See the attached base environment package list for the full ~700 packages
  that are installed and the versions we are currently installing
- Add your package to Conda Forge if it is not present
- [Open
  issues](https://gitlab.ewi.tudelft.nl/bhmgerritsen/anaconda-dee-config/-/issues)
  on Gitlab with your concerns and requests
- Share this email with your colleagues who may be interested
- And, please give a hand so we can share the workload of this task
